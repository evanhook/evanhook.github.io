About Me

Working in marketing means that I get to collaborate with a ton of different teams throughout the company. A friend told me about Epicodus and I attended a workshop to learn more. I was immediately excited by the things that we were learning and decided to enroll in the evening program to continue learning and (hopefully) make a career change!
